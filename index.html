Spinach, Tofu and Sesame Stir-Fry

By: Martha Rose Shulman

Yield: 3 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • ½ pound tofu, cut in small dice
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated or minced fresh ginger
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes
  • Soy sauce to taste
  • 16-ounce bag baby spinach, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Preparation

Step 1: Heat the canola oil over medium-high heat in a large nonstick skillet or wok, and add the tofu. Stir-fry until the tofu is lightly colored, about 3–5 minutes. Add the garlic, ginger, and chili flakes. Cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 1 minute, then add soy sauce to taste. Add the spinach and stir-fry until the spinach wilts, about 1 minute. Stir in the sesame seeds and add more soy sauce to taste. Remove from the heat.

Step 2: Using tongs, transfer the spinach and tofu mixture to a serving bowl, leaving the liquid behind in the pan. Drizzle with the sesame oil, and add more soy sauce as desired. Serve with rice, grains, or noodles, or use as a filling for whole wheat pita bread.

Tip: This is a last-minute preparation, but you can have your ingredients prepared well in advance.
